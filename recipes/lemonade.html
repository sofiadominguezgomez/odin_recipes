Lemonade Recipe

Hello there! This page will walk you through the process of making a very refreshing jug of lemonade! Now let's get started, and good luck!

Ingredients

      6 lemons
      Water
      Ice cubes
      Sugar

Steps

      Cut the lemons in halves and squeeze them
      Add the juice to your jug with 2 teablespoons of sugar and mix them until the sugar completely disolves
      Add your ice subes and finally top it off with water

Succes! You could add some meant leaves if you have any or leave it like this, enjoy!
