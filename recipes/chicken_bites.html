Chicken bites Recipe

Hello there! This page will walk you through the process of making a very basic dish of chicken bites that work perfectly with plain rice! Now let's get started, and good luck!

Ingredients

      1 chciekn breast
      Salt, pepper, garlic powder, curcuma, pimenton ahumado o dulce
      1 or 2 cloves of garlic
      1 onion
      Oil

Steps

      First, chop the garlic and the onion into tiny pieces
      Add a teablespoon of oil to a pot and turn the heat on, then the chopped garlic and onion
      While that cooks, cut the chicken breast into cubes the size of a bite
      Add a teablespoon of oil and the spices, make sure that you mix everything thoroughly
      When the onion looks sort of transparent, add the chicken and combine
      After some minutes, turn around the chicken so that iit coks on all sides
      Make sure that it is not raw at the medium and you are done!

Succes! You could add the to a salad, eat them with vegetables or plain rice. Maybe all of them!
