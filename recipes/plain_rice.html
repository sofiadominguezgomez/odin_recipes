Plain Rice Recipe

Hello there! This page will walk you through the process of making a very basic dish of plain rice, perfect companion for nearly everything! Now let's get started, and good luck!

Ingredients

      1 cup of rice
      3 cups of water
      1 or 2 cloves of garlic
      1 onion
      Some oil and salt

Steps

      First, chop the garlic and the onion into tiny pieces
      Add a teablespoon of oil to a pot and turn the heat on, then the chopped garlic and onion
      When the onion looks sort of transparent, add the rice and combine thoroughly
      After a minute or two, add the water. This could also be some broth to add flavor
      Put the lid on top of the pot. Leave it at medium heat for 20 minutes. Then turn off the heat and leave it for 10 minutes

Succes! You have plain rice to accompany any meal that you want. This is obviously the most basic way of preparing it, but you could add spices or othe vegetables of your liking to change the final product, never be afraid of trying something new!
